[Heading 1] ABSTRACT
[Normal (Web)] The Virtual Support System for Emotional Wellness is a web-based application designed to assist users in managing emotional imbalances and mood fluctuations through AI-driven analysis and personalized recommendations. In an era where emotional health is often compromised due to stress, social isolation, or lack of timely support, this system offers a proactive digital solution to promote mental well-being. Leveraging the capabilities of artificial intelligence, the application utilizes language processing models to interpret user-inputted mood data and generate contextually appropriate suggestions aimed at emotional improvement. These may include motivational prompts, mindfulness exercises, and mood-boosting activities. The system also tracks mood history and visualizes emotional trends over time, enabling users to gain deeper insights into their emotional patterns. Developed using HTML and CSS for the frontend, PHP for server-side scripting, and MySQL for secure data storage, the platform incorporates essential features such as user authentication, an interactive dashboard, progress tracking, and suggestion history. The integration of ChatGPT enhances the system’s ability to deliver dynamic, empathetic, and intelligent responses based on user context. Future upgrades will include a mobile-friendly version, voice assistant integration, push notifications for wellness reminders, and enhanced data privacy protocols. By merging emotional intelligence with modern web technologies, this project presents an innovative framework to support digital mental wellness, making emotional care more accessible, responsive, and personalized.
